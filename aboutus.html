About Us

At Emerald Aire, our goal is to win loyal fans to our team.  Take a minute to watch our video below.

History

Our company was formed as a corporation in February of 1985. Its primary business was HVAC service, maintenance, refrigeration and gas piping. The original founders were Ron Richards, Russ Hunter, and Doug Happe. Both Ron and Russ retired and Russ passed away in 2002. Doug is the remaining founder and current president of the corporation.

In 1995, Emerald Aire merged with Sound Air, a local sheet metal fabrication and installation company. Linda Dupuis-Fricke was the sole owner of Sound Air and for a while became president of the new company, until she left the in 1999.  Doug subsequently became the majority stock holder, while John Ridge purchased Russ’s old stock. Doug and John are currently the primary stock holders of the company.

January 1, 2000 marked the beginning of a new millennium, as well as a new era at Emerald Aire. The corporate structure was dissolved and rebuilt as a team organization. This was accompanied by a renewed dedication to our core philosophies of excellence, integrity, and service.

The VRF Experts

Today Emerald Aire is one of the largest HVAC companies in the NW.  We regularly work in every part of the industry: Commercial, Residential, Service, Planned Maintenance, Refrigeration, Controls, and Sheet Metal Fabrication.

We are the VRF experts and have achieved status as one of Mitsubishi's Diamond Distributors, a distinction held by only 8 other companies in the nation.  Although we handle jobs of all sizes, we are experts in the VRF systems that are becoming more popular as stricter energy requirements and efficiencies are now being required by building codes everywhere.

Team Structure

At Emerald Aire, Team is not a catchy buzzword.  We mean it.  Our coaches are devoted to making sure that everyone brings their 'A' game to work, because that's what our customers deserve.
