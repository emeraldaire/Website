Projects

We're proud of the work we do for our customers. Emerald Aire has worked on some of the largest HVAC projects in the Pacific NW. Our work has tackled large commercial mixed-use projects, education facilities, and large public works projects where we've partnered with other contractors. Every job presents a unique set of challenges to be solved by our outstanding engineering and construction teams.

With over 35 years of experience in the industry, we're known for our professionalism and ability to work well with other trades. Because our process, planning, and project management are so effective, we are often asked to coordinate the other building trades to insure a seamless productive timeline.
